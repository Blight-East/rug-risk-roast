Risk language

Educational review, not a guarantee.

Rug-Risk Roast is an educational trust-readiness review, not financial, legal, tax, investment, or compliance advice. We do not guarantee token success, price performance, liquidity, listings, community growth, legal safety, or buyer trust.

What we do

  • Review public launch presentation.
  • Surface operational red flags.
  • Rewrite unclear public copy.
  • Point out missing disclosures.
  • Recommend clearer safety language.

What we do not do

  • We do not certify launches.
  • We do not guarantee buyer trust.
  • We do not provide legal compliance opinions.
  • We do not help hide wallets, supply, risks, or affiliations.
  • We do not help create fake volume, fake holders, fake engagement, or misleading claims.

Language to avoid

Avoid claims like guaranteed, safe, certified, next 100x, floor will hold, buy now, legal compliance guaranteed, investment-ready, or anything encouraging coordinated price action.
