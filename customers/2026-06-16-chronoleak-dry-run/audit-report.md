Verdict: Weak trust posture. ## Top 10 Red Flags

1. High - "Early holders" language implies timing advantage. Replace it with neutral launch-status language.
2. High - "Community will hold the floor" sounds like coordinated price support. Remove floor/holding language entirely.
3. High - No authority status is prepared. Add mint/freeze/update authority status or say it is unknown.
4. High - No reserve or supply disclosure exists. State whether the creator keeps any allocation.
5. Medium - "Official timeline coin" sounds bigger than the project can prove. Use "experimental Solana meme" instead.
6. Medium - Telegram pin has no official mint, official links, or DM scam warning. Add all three before launch.
7. Medium - Launch post leads with urgency instead of the concept. Explain what it is before asking people to care.
8. Medium - No website or social consistency yet. Token page, X bio, and Telegram pin should use the same one-line description.
9. Low - The time/timeline theme is good but generic. Add one concrete line of personality so it does not feel AI-generated.
10. Low - No post-launch cadence is stated. Prepare 3-5 non-price updates for the first 24 hours.
