# Rug-Risk Roast Audit Framework

Score each section from 0 to 10. Add the section scores and convert to a 100-point total.

## Score Bands

- 0-39: High-risk public presentation. Do not launch before fixing basics.
- 40-59: Weak trust posture. Launch may look rushed or extractive.
- 60-74: Acceptable but fragile. Fix high-severity gaps before promotion.
- 75-89: Strong public presentation. Remaining issues are mostly polish.
- 90-100: Rare. Clean, clear, transparent, and consistent.

## Severity

- Critical: can create direct scam, wallet, legal, or identity risk.
- High: likely to make serious buyers/community members distrust the launch.
- Medium: hurts credibility or clarity but is fixable.
- Low: polish issue.

## Scoring Categories

### 1. Identity Clarity

Measures whether a stranger can understand what the project is in 5 seconds.

High score:
- Name, ticker, concept, and audience are obvious.
- No accidental affiliation with serious brands or protected IP.
- Anonymous/faceless status is disclosed without sounding evasive.

Low score:
- Confusing name/ticker.
- Looks like a copy of another brand.
- Uses private/business identity by accident.
- No clear reason to exist.

### 2. Token / Page Copy

Measures clarity, accuracy, and risk level of token description, website copy, and launch page.

High score:
- Plain explanation.
- No profit claims.
- No fake utility.
- No fake roadmap.
- Clear official links.

Low score:
- Hype without specifics.
- "100x", "moon", "send it", "guaranteed", "safe", "certified".
- Copy implies investment upside.
- Missing risk language.

### 3. Visual Trust

Measures whether assets look original, clear, and non-deceptive.

High score:
- Original token image.
- Readable at small size.
- No stolen logos, creator likenesses, or copyrighted characters.
- Visual system matches the concept.

Low score:
- Looks AI-generic, copied, low-effort, or stolen.
- Uses real brands, celebrities, show marks, or protected characters.
- Visual says one thing while copy says another.

### 4. Social Proof

Measures social setup and public consistency.

High score:
- X, Telegram, website, and launch page match.
- Pinned message explains risk and official links.
- No fake followers, bots, or copied engagement.

Low score:
- Blank socials.
- Mismatched links.
- No pinned warning.
- Bot-like comments.
- Serious brand account used for anonymous launch.

### 5. Wallet / Liquidity Disclosure

Measures transparency around supply, reserve, authorities, and LP.

High score:
- Mint/freeze/update authority status posted.
- Reserve wallet disclosed if any.
- LP/pool link posted if applicable.
- No hidden team allocation.

Low score:
- Unexplained supply/reserve.
- "LP burned/locked" claim without proof.
- Retained supply not disclosed.
- Authority status unknown or hidden.

### 6. Launch Safety

Measures operational readiness.

High score:
- Clean burner wallet.
- Correct official links.
- Scam warning prepared.
- Screenshots/records saved.
- Launch sequence documented.

Low score:
- Rushed wallet prompts.
- No test transaction.
- No emergency plan.
- No official-link discipline.

### 7. Community Readiness

Measures whether the project can handle first 24 hours.

High score:
- Telegram rules pinned.
- Moderator plan.
- Content queued.
- FUD responses ready.
- Organic engagement exists.

Low score:
- Empty Telegram.
- No content plan.
- Price-only community language.
- No moderation.

### 8. Scam-Link Risk

Measures how easily users can be tricked.

High score:
- Official links centralized.
- No airdrop/claim links.
- DM warning pinned.
- Impersonator response prepared.

Low score:
- Multiple unofficial links.
- No mint clarity.
- No pinned official link.
- Allows random links in Telegram.

### 9. Compliance Language

Measures whether public language avoids obvious liability and platform risk.

High score:
- Education/meme/tooling language.
- Risk disclaimers.
- No advice or guarantees.
- No creator endorsement claims.

Low score:
- Profit promises.
- "Invest now."
- "Guaranteed."
- "Legal/safe/certified."
- Any language encouraging manipulation.

## Required Report Sections

Every report must include:

- Overall score.
- Section scores.
- Top 10 red flags.
- Severity per issue.
- Fixed token description.
- Fixed X bio.
- Fixed pinned Telegram post.
- Fixed launch post.
- "Do not say this" list.
- Next 5 actions.
