# Quick Intake Questions

Use this short intake for Tally or Google Forms. The buyer should be able to finish it on mobile in under 2 minutes.

## Required

1. Contact email, Telegram, or X
2. Payment transaction signature
3. Project name + ticker
4. Live or pre-launch? Launching where?
5. Paste everything you want roasted

Prompt for field 5:

```text
Paste whatever you have: token/page link, website, X, Telegram, Discord, token description, X bio, Telegram pin, launch post, wallet/liquidity notes, authority status, or rough drafts. If something is not ready yet, just say not ready.
```

## Optional

6. What feels sketchiest or most urgent?

## Required Checkbox

```text
I understand this is an educational trust/readiness review, not financial, legal, tax, investment, or launch-success advice.
```
